Sung Jinwoo

  • Age: 20
  • Power Level: Limitless
  • Affiliation: Guild Master

About Sung Jinwoo

Sung Jinwoo, the main character of "Solo Leveling," is known for his immense strength and extraordinary growth. Once a weak hunter, he becomes one of the strongest individuals in his world after undergoing a transformation. As the Guild Master, he commands tremendous respect.

Ryomen Sukuna

  • Age: Unknown (Immortal)
  • Power Level: God-like
  • Affiliation: Curse King

About Sukuna

Ryomen Sukuna, known as the King of Curses, is one of the most powerful curses in the world of "Jujutsu Kaisen." His power and ruthlessness make him a formidable enemy. Though once a human, Sukuna's curse form is nearly immortal and uncontrollable.

Satoru Gojo

  • Age: 28
  • Power Level: Limitless
  • Affiliation: Jujutsu High

About Gojo

Satoru Gojo is a jujutsu sorcerer and one of the most powerful characters in "Jujutsu Kaisen." With his Limitless technique and Six Eyes, Gojo is considered an unbeatable force. He is a teacher at Jujutsu High, training the next generation of sorcerers.
